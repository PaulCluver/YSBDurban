An art of striking while moving

which employs intricate kicking, throwing, and grappling techniques which are set up by strikes executed as the practitioner moves around the opponent in a circular fashion.

Ancient Roots / modern application

the fighting theory, application, developmental methods, and outward appearance of this art all originate from and accord with the Chinese classic Book of Changes (周易)

Our Mission

is to train hardworking individuals to attain proficiency in the martial art of Yin Style Baguazhang. We provide systematic instruction to help you attain the "cold, crisp, and fast" fighting methods of this art.

Find out more about Yin Style Bagua

A Fighting Art

Developed in accord with the symbolism and meaning of the eight trigrams in the Book of Changes, Yin Style Bagua has eight distinct systems within the art. Each of these systems, represented by a trigram and particular animal, stands on its own as a complete martial art with unique developmental and fighting methods.

Our Aim

To provide systematic instruction to motivated, hard working people in the Durban area who want to learn the "cold, crisp, and fast" fighting methods of this combative art.
